Hyperlinks

Theres 2 different hyperlinks

1.Internal Links
Syntax: <a herf = #link_within_document>Click Me </a >
Must include.
<a id ="link_within_document">Destination <a>
Example: Click Me to go down.

2.External Links
Syntax: <a herf = External_url>Click Me </a >
Example: Click Me to open Google.
